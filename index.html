🤖 How the Bot Works

The model, the method, and the money management behind every MLB pick.

⚾
MLB Only — All Season
All real-money picks target MLB totals (over/under) and run lines (-1.5/+1.5). The bot runs every day of the MLB season, analyzing every game on the slate.
🧢
Pitcher-Centric Model
The mathematical baseline is built around the confirmed starting pitcher's ERA, combined with team offense, park factors, and a Monte Carlo simulation of 10,000 game outcomes.
🌦️
Weather & Situational AI
On top of the math, an AI layer researches live weather (wind direction, temperature), bullpen fatigue, lineup injuries, platoon matchups, and travel to fine-tune each projection.
🏟️
30-Park Park Factors
Every park has a custom run factor baked in — from Coors Field (1.34×) to Oracle Park (0.93×). Wind blowing out at Wrigley is not the same as a dome game in Tampa.
⚡
Edge Score
Every pick shows an Edge score — the gap between the model's adjusted projection and the Vegas line. The bot only bets when the edge clears a minimum threshold.
💰
Flat Betting System
$25 per bet, every bet at standard -110 odds. No parlays. No chasing. No varying stakes. A 52.4% win rate breaks even — the bot targets 55%+ to stay profitable long term.
